Welcome!

You have been invited to take part in a research study being conducted by the Computational Memory Lab at the University of Pennsylvania. In this study, you will see cards containing words and later be asked to judge how recently you saw them. Full instructions will be provided before the study.

The experiment will last approximately 45-60 minutes, not counting breaks. The only minor risk of the study is fatigue from completing the task. There will be opportunities to rest between trials, in case you need to take a break at any point.

Your participation is completely voluntary, and you may stop the experiment at any time. If you have any questions about your rights as a research participant, or about this study in general, please contact the principal investigator:
